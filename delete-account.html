Delete Your Account

Last updated: June 15, 2026

You can delete your Bazmi Voice Chat account and associated personal data at any time. This page explains how to do it and what happens to your information.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (fastest)

  1. Open the Bazmi app and go to your Profile.
  2. Tap Settings (the gear icon).
  3. Tap Account → Delete Account.
  4. Confirm. Your account will be scheduled for deletion.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can't access the app, email us from the address or phone associated with your account and we'll process your request:

Send an email to support@bazmivoicechat.com with the subject "Delete My Account" and include your username / Bazmi ID and the phone number or sign-in method you used. We may ask you to verify ownership before deleting.

What gets deleted

When your deletion is processed, we delete or anonymize the personal data associated with your account, including:

  • Your profile (name, username, photo, bio, country, gender).
  • Your account identifiers (phone/social/guest login).
  • Your room, chat history, reactions, and activity tied to your account.
  • Your owned cosmetics and any remaining virtual items (see below).

What may be retained

We may retain a limited amount of data for a period after deletion where necessary to:

  • Comply with legal, tax, or accounting obligations (e.g., records of purchases or payouts).
  • Prevent fraud and abuse, and enforce our Terms and bans.
  • Resolve disputes or respond to lawful requests.

Such data is kept only as long as required and is then deleted or anonymized.

Virtual items & purchases

Deleting your account forfeits any remaining diamonds/coins, gifts, and cosmetics. These have no monetary value and are non-refundable except where required by law or the relevant app store's policy. Manage subscription/purchase history through your Apple App Store or Google Play account.

Timing

Account deletion is typically completed within 30 days of a verified request. Some backups and legally-retained records may persist for a limited additional period as described above.

Need help?

Contact support@bazmivoicechat.com and we'll be glad to assist. See our Privacy Policy for more on how we handle your data.
